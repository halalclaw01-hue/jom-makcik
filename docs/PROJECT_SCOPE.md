# Project Scope

## Jom Makcik CareRide MVP

Jom Makcik CareRide is a minimum viable product for a controlled assisted mobility platform.

The MVP supports coordinated transport and care assistance for passengers who need supervised mobility help, such as senior citizens, patients, or dependents traveling to medical appointments or similar care-related destinations.

## Not Open E-Hailing

This platform is not open e-hailing.

It must not behave like an uncontrolled driver marketplace. Riders cannot freely receive jobs without platform approval, and sensitive operations must remain under admin oversight.

## Controlled Assisted Mobility

The platform is designed around controlled assisted mobility:

- Passenger bookings are tracked through approved booking statuses.
- Payment proof is checked manually before trip matching.
- Riders must be approved before receiving jobs.
- Admins can monitor bookings, rider assignment, payment status, chat, and care reports.

## Admin Oversight

Admin oversight is required for the MVP.

Admins must be able to:

- Review bookings.
- Approve payment proof.
- Approve or reject riders.
- Assign riders.
- Monitor chat.
- Manage trip and care report visibility.

## Payment Scope

The MVP uses manual payment proof first.

No real payment gateway should be connected yet. Passengers upload payment proof, and an admin must approve it before the booking can move forward.

## Rider Approval Scope

Riders must be approved before receiving jobs.

New rider accounts or rider profiles must not be allowed to receive job offers until an admin approves them.
